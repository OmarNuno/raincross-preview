Dr. Custode brings a rare depth of clinical and athletic expertise. He has treated professional athletes, bodybuilders, collegiate and high school competitors, dancers, and gymnasts — and he applies that same level of attention to every patient who walks through the door.

01
Back & Lower Back Pain
One of the most common reasons patients seek chiropractic care — we address the root cause, not just the symptom.
Spinal adjustments, targeted traction, and corrective exercises work together to relieve compression, restore alignment, and reduce muscle tension. Dr. Custode reviews imaging when needed for a precise diagnosis.

02
Neck Pain & Stiffness
Screen time, stress, and poor posture are driving an epidemic of cervical pain — chiropractic offers lasting relief.
Gentle cervical adjustments and soft tissue work restore mobility, reduce nerve pressure, and address the postural patterns causing the problem. Tech neck and whiplash respond especially well.
CervicalTech NeckWhiplash
03
Sciatica & Nerve Pain
Sharp, burning pain radiating down the leg — sciatic compression often responds very well to chiropractic care.
Spinal adjustments and traction techniques decompress the affected nerve roots and restore normal biomechanics, reducing pain and preventing recurrence without surgery or medication.
SciaticNerve PainDisc Herniation
04
Headaches & Migraines
Many chronic headaches originate in the cervical spine — treating the source, not masking the symptom.
Cervicogenic headaches caused by spinal dysfunction respond strongly to chiropractic adjustments. Even some migraines can decrease in frequency and severity with consistent care.
TensionCervicogenicMigraine
05
Sports Injuries
Shoulder, knee, ankle — Dr. Custode's athletic background means sport-specific treatment from day one.
Accurate diagnosis, targeted manual therapy, structured rehab, and a clear return-to-sport timeline. We've treated athletes from high school to professional level across dozens of sports.
ShoulderKneeAnkleRehab
06
Auto Accident Injuries
Whiplash and soft tissue injuries often appear days later — early chiropractic intervention is critical.
We provide thorough post-accident evaluation, documentation, and structured treatment. We work with auto insurance providers and can coordinate with legal counsel for personal injury cases.
