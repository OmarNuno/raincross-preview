Conditions We Treat

Common Conditions Helped by Chiropractic

Not sure if chiropractic care is right for your condition? Here's a look at what we commonly treat — and how we approach each one.

Lower back pain is one of the most common reasons people seek chiropractic care. Whether it's from prolonged sitting, a muscle strain, or a structural issue like a herniated disc, chiropractic adjustments can help restore proper alignment, reduce nerve irritation, and relieve muscle tension.

What We Do

Dr. Custode performs a thorough evaluation — including postural assessment and, when needed, review of imaging — to identify the root cause. Treatment may include spinal adjustments, targeted traction, corrective exercises, and ergonomic guidance.

Lumbar Pain Disc Issues Muscle Strain Postural Issues

Neck pain is increasingly common — driven by screen time, poor posture, stress, and minor trauma. Left untreated, it can lead to headaches, shoulder tension, and arm tingling. Chiropractic care targets the cervical spine to restore mobility and reduce pressure on surrounding structures.

What We Do

Gentle cervical adjustments, soft tissue work, and postural retraining help relieve stiffness and pain while addressing the underlying cause — not just the symptoms.

Cervical Pain Tech Neck Whiplash Stiffness

Sciatica occurs when the sciatic nerve — running from the lower back through the hips and down each leg — becomes compressed or irritated. Symptoms range from a dull ache to sharp, burning pain or numbness along the leg. Chiropractic care can reduce the compression causing sciatic symptoms without surgery or medication.

What We Do

Spinal adjustments, specific traction techniques, and targeted exercises work to decompress the affected nerve roots and restore normal movement patterns.

Sciatic Nerve Leg Pain Numbness Disc Herniation

Many chronic headaches originate in the cervical spine or from muscle tension in the neck and shoulders. Cervicogenic headaches — those caused by spinal dysfunction — respond especially well to chiropractic treatment. Even some migraines can be reduced in frequency and intensity with regular adjustments.

What We Do

We assess cervical alignment and muscle tension patterns to determine if spinal dysfunction is contributing to your headaches and create a targeted treatment plan.

Tension Headaches Cervicogenic Migraine Support

From rotator cuff strains to IT band syndrome to ankle sprains, sports injuries involve complex interactions between bones, muscles, ligaments, and nerves. Dr. Custode's extensive experience with athletes at all levels — from high school to professional — means your injury gets a diagnosis-first, sport-specific treatment approach.

What We Do

Accurate diagnosis, targeted manual therapy, rehab protocols, and a clear return-to-play timeline are the cornerstones of sports injury care at Raincross Chiropractic.

Rotator Cuff Knee Pain Ankle Sprains Athletic Rehab

Auto accidents often cause whiplash, soft tissue injuries, and spinal misalignments that aren't immediately painful — but develop into chronic issues over weeks. Early chiropractic intervention after a car accident can prevent long-term damage and help document injuries for insurance purposes.

What We Do

We provide thorough post-accident evaluation, documentation, and a structured treatment plan. We work with your auto insurance provider and can coordinate care with your attorney if needed.

Whiplash Auto Accident Soft Tissue Insurance

Prolonged sitting, device use, and muscle imbalances can cause significant postural distortion over time. Left unaddressed, poor posture accelerates spinal degeneration and leads to chronic pain. Mild to moderate scoliosis can also be managed with chiropractic to reduce progression and discomfort.

What We Do

Spinal remodeling techniques, corrective exercise programs, and specific traction are used to gradually improve curvature and posture — with measurable progress over time.
