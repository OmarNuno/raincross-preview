About
the Doctor

Dr. Custode combines rare clinical depth with real-world athletic experience. He has treated professional athletes, bodybuilders, collegiate and high school competitors, dancers, and gymnasts — and brings that same precision to every patient.

His approach: identify the structural root cause, not just manage symptoms. Treatment addresses posture, muscular imbalance, and spinal alignment alongside the immediate complaint.
